**changelog:** `12/23/2025`
> ## Menu
> - **Slider Manual Input** - Ctrl+Click on any slider to type a value directly.
> - **Hex Color Input** - Color pickers now have a hex input field. Type colors like `#FF5500` or `#FF5500FF` (with alpha) and press Enter.

> ## Misc
> - **Improved Knifebot** - Now uses optimal 2 slash + 1 stab combo (123 damage, kills 100hp). Detects backstabs for instant kills. Fixed separate cooldowns for slash/stab attacks.

> ## Fixes
> - **Knifebot Stability** - Added null pointer checks to prevent crashes.
> - **Weather Effects** - Temporarily disabled (Ash/Snow/Stars) due to missing particle files.
