**changelog:** `12/23/2025`
> ## Radar Improvements
> - **C4 Tracking** - New options to show dropped and planted C4 on radar with bomb icon.
> - **C4 Timer Colors** - Planted bomb icon changes from yellow → orange → red (pulsing) based on time remaining.
> - **C4 Timer Display** - Optional timer text next to planted bomb, auto-hides when defused/exploded.
> - **Enemy Death Markers** - Track enemy deaths on radar with optional name display.
> - **Skull Icon** - Death markers now use skull-crack icon, tinted blue (team) or red (enemy).
